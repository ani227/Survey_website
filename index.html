About Survey

We as Indians are very much fond of fixing problems that occur in our day to day lives, irrespective of whether the problem belongs to us, our acquaintances or the most important, our beloved Nation.

Not to forget that we also enjoy unity in diversity, because together we are builders, creators, motivators, artists, producers, consumers, risk-takers, problem-solvers, and in the process tend to become the nation builders. Some amongst us are on a never ending quest to improve the lives of others, and with a dream to make a positive impact in everyone's lives are toiling day and night both to scratch their own itch, and to solve the problems.

From the power set of impact makers, we come as a small group of problem-solvers and risk-takers who wish to fix a lot of problems that are prevalent in our country, but being a smaller subset we are determined to fix them bit-by-bit.

With this dedication and lots of enthusiasm we are trying to know how Indians reads the news or the related information of the events that are happening around in their lives. As we all know that media is the 4th pillar of democracy, we wish to know whether it is living up to the expectations of the citizens and maintaining its credibility or not, because if we get to know the right problems only then we can solve it. In order to promote the survey so that it reaches masses and majority of people across the nation fills it we are running a parallel lucky draw as well.

Therefore, we humbly request you to kindly take part in this survey and help us get to know the challenges faced by you, because if we won't think about our nation, who else will!?
